How This App Works

Go to Library

This app lets you save useful links, organize them in your library, and quickly share them. It can also generate QR codes for your URLs so you can open them on your phone fast.

Step 1
Create an account

Go to /signup/ to make a new account, or log in at /login/.

Step 2
Add URLs

Use /add/ to save a new link to your library.

Step 3
View your library

Open /library/ to see every saved link. You can search, edit, or delete items.

Step 4
Create QR codes

The project includes QR code support (using qrcode and Pillow). When enabled on a link, a QR image is saved under /media/ and served at {{ MEDIA_URL }} in development.
